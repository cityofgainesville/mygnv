Draft Housing Action Plan



A draft Housing Action Plan is available for review online. The draft includes recommendations that offer eight strategies to address the challenges the City of Gainesville faces with affordable housing development including the financing and ensuring long-term affordability of units.

To schedule a neighborhood or group meeting, or to provide feedback, please contact Phimetto Lewis at 393-8862. A telephone town hall is also being scheduled.
